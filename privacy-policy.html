Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TITAN AI ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our social media management service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Account Information: When you connect your TikTok account via OAuth, we receive your TikTok open_id, display name, and profile picture.
  • Content Data: Videos, images, captions, and scheduling information you create or upload through our platform.
  • Usage Data: Log data, device information, browser type, and interaction patterns with our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our social media management services
  • Authenticate your TikTok account via TikTok Login Kit
  • Schedule and publish content to TikTok on your behalf
  • Send you service-related notifications and updates
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With TikTok's API as necessary to provide our services
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With your consent or at your direction

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with TikTok's Developer API. Your use of TikTok is subject to TikTok's own Privacy Policy. We are not responsible for TikTok's data practices.
